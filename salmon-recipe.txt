Ingredients:
 - 1 salmon fillet
 - salt, pepper
 - butter, mustard

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C. 
 2. Use the butter and mustard to create a sauce to put over 
 3. If the salmon is frozen, put it in the microwave for 2-3 min. After that put salt, pepper and some lemon. After that put some of the sauce in it.
 4. After 20-25 min check if the salmon is ready
